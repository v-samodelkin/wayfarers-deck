# Three sample quests

These complete samples show the card structure and the range of Wayfarer's Deck. The paid deck contains 24 cards balanced across six quest types and four danger tiers.

## The Lantern Orchard

**Type:** Explore · **Danger:** 1/4

**Hook:** Each dusk, blue lantern-fruit reveals a path that vanishes before sunrise.

**Objective:** Map the hidden path and recover a seed from its final tree.

**Twist:** Picking any other fruit changes the route behind the party.

**Reward:** A seed that grows one night of safe, revealing light.

## Seven Empty Chairs

**Type:** Mystery · **Danger:** 2/4

**Hook:** Every morning, another chair appears around the mayor's dining table.

**Objective:** Identify the expected guests before the seventh chair is occupied.

**Twist:** The chairs belong to forgotten founders whose stolen names hide in local songs.

**Reward:** A place in the town archive and a favor from the restored spirits.

## Night of the Second Sun

**Type:** Survival · **Danger:** 4/4

**Hook:** A pale second sun rises at midnight and begins bleaching color, warmth, and memory from the land.

**Objective:** Preserve a sanctuary and endure until the false sun can be eclipsed.

**Twist:** The necessary eclipse requires someone to carry the sanctuary's last true flame outside.

**Reward:** A vial of dawnlight that restores lost color and one faded memory.
